About the Event

Get Ready For An Unforgettable Experience!

Welcome to the East Coast Inflate-A-Palooza! Get ready for an unforgettable experience at the Garrison Grounds, where we'll host Atlantic Canada's Largest Inflatable Extravaganza. Join us for a fun-filled festival featuring a wide array of inflatable attractions, delicious food vendors, and live entertainment.

For the third year in a row, you will get to see 50+ inflatables at the same time! Our Giant Inflatable Characters & displays will take over the Garrison Grounds along with 25+ diverse games and inflatables — ranging from bouncy castles to slides, obstacle courses, mechanical rides and carnival games — you name it!

Even the little ones can catch our train for a ride around the grounds! With such variety, there's ample entertainment for children and grown-ups alike, ensuring everyone can enjoy themselves outdoors in the fresh air.

What Awaits You

Featured Attractions

From towering rock walls to massive obstacle courses, there's something for every thrill seeker and every age group!

30' Rock Wall

Test your climbing skills on our massive 30-foot rock wall. A towering challenge for adventurers of all ages!

170' Obstacle Course

Race through our legendary 170-foot obstacle course! Climb, crawl, slide, and bounce your way to the finish line.

The Eliminator

Think you've got what it takes? Challenge your friends on The Eliminator — a gladiator-style battle on inflatable pedestals!

30+ Inflatable Displays

Wander through our incredible collection of giant inflatable characters and displays — perfect for photos and unforgettable memories!

Food Vendors

Fuel up at our delicious food village! Cotton candy, popcorn, hot dogs, and many more nostalgic carnival treats from 10+ vendors.

25+ Games & Activities

Bouncy castles, slides, mechanical rides, carnival games, and a kids' train ride around the grounds. Fun never stops!

Plan Your Visit

Know Before You Go

Everything you need to know to make the most of your Inflate-a-Palooza experience.

What to Wear

Comfortable clothes and socks — you'll be climbing, sliding & bouncing! Apply sunscreen and stay hydrated on sunny days.

Age Policy

Fun for ages 0–100! Kids 12 and under must be accompanied by an adult (max 4 kids per adult). Children under the age of 2 are free!

Parking

Pay & display parking is available in the lot adjacent to Garrison Grounds. Street parking is also available nearby.

Food & Treats

10+ food vendors on site will keep you fuelled. Each vendor accepts their own payment — cash and card accepted.

Weather Policy

Events are weather-dependent. Check our social media for real-time updates. We have shaded areas throughout the site.

Time Slots

Daily sessions: 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM and 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM. Choose the right slot when booking your tickets!
